About

I am a Data Science Manager at Apple, where I lead the experimentation science team for Apple Ads. My team develops experiment designs, metrics, and statistical analyses to measure the impact of new algorithms and features on business metrics. My professional work centers on online experimentation and causal inference ‚Äî establishing causality through both randomized experiments and observational data.

My interest in applying quantitative methods to societal challenges was shaped early ‚Äî growing up in India with a father who is a practicing cardiologist, I saw firsthand the impact of medicine on human life. This led me to a computer science research lab focused on bioinformatics and public health. Under Dr. Armin Mikler and Dr. Chetan Tiwari at the University of North Texas, my doctoral research focused on location-allocation algorithms for emergency response planning, disease modeling, and computational epidemiology.

Before Apple, I was a Senior Data Scientist at Twitter, working on experimentation, customer retention modeling, and variance reduction methods for A/B testing.
